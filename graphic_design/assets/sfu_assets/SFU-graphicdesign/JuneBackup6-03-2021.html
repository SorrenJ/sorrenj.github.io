International Graduate Students

Reminder to register your international travel plan with SFU: If you are currently outside of Canada, and plan to travel or return to Canada, make sure to register your international arrival plans with SFU ahead of your flight. University will be in touch with the students who registered their plans and will continue to support them as they travel to Canada. If you need support in completing the required travel plans or have any questions about self-isolation supports, please reach out to student_support@sfu.ca. Advisors are available to support you.

This workshop is open to all SFU students.

RSVP here. Open to graduate students only.
